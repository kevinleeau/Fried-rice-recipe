Easy Fried Rice

Fried rice ingredients

  • White rice, jasmine or long-grain; fresh or leftover
  • Minced white onion
  • Diced carrots
  • Green peas, fresh or frozen (avoid canned; too mushy)
  • Toasted sesame oil
  • Eggs whisked
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and white pepper (spicy flavor and adds no color to the dish)
How to make it?
  1. Fry the rice by lightly browning in a pan or wok.
  2. Stir-fry the onions, garlic, and carrots until tender.
  3. Make a large well in the center of the pan with the rice.
  4. Pour in the whisked eggs and scramble until small curds form.
  5. Soy sauce is optional but adds a savory flavor to the rice.
  6. Add green peas at the end to retain color and shape.
